Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: February 7, 2026

🔍
Information We Collect
• Account Information: Email address, display name, and authentication data • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings you choose to process (stored locally by default) • Transcriptions: Text converted from your audio recordings • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, and performance metrics • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version • Location Data: Only if explicitly granted for meeting location features (optional) We do NOT collect: • Personal conversations unless you explicitly record them • Background audio when the app is not in use • Contacts, photos, or other personal files without permission
⚙
How We Use Your Information
• Provide Core Services: Process audio, generate transcriptions and summaries • Improve User Experience: Enhance app functionality and performance • Account Management: Authenticate users and sync data across devices • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and troubleshoot issues • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect user rights • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats We will NEVER: • Sell your personal information to third parties • Use your recordings for advertising purposes • Share your content without explicit consent • Access your recordings without your permission
🔒
Data Storage and Security
• Local Storage: Audio recordings and transcriptions are stored locally on your device by default • Cloud Storage: Optional premium feature with end-to-end encryption • Data Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access your data • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review and update our security measures • Data Minimization: We only collect and store data necessary for app functionality Your recordings remain private and are never accessed for training AI models without explicit consent.
🌐
Third-Party Services
VoiceSketch integrates with the following third-party services: • OpenAI: For text summarization and image generation (content is processed but not stored) • Firebase (Google): For authentication, cloud storage, and analytics • Apple Services: For in-app purchases and speech recognition We ensure all partners meet our privacy and security standards.
